The Absolute Best Lasagna Recipe

Seriously, this will be best Lasagna you'll ever eat!

This is a recipe that's been passed down in my family for generations. I'll keep it short and sweet for you. You only really need a couple ingredients.

  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 3 tbsp of oregano
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1 tsp of crushed red pepper
  • 2 packets of McCormick Mushroom Spaghetti seasoning
  • Onion powder or diced onion
  • Green pepper (optional)
  • 16 oz Ricotta cheese
  • Your choice of Lasagna Noodles
  • 10 oz bag of fresh mozerella cheese

The prep is pretty simple. If you've never cooked lasagna before that's okay. Just follow the directions below:

  1. Brown the meat in a pan or skillet, add onion or onion powder and diced green pepper.
  2. Begin boiling lasagna noodles
  3. Add both cans of tomato sauce, stir until fully mixed.
  4. Add both packets of seasoning mix.
  5. Stir until mixed. Simmer.
  6. Add Ricotta cheese and crushed red pepper.
  7. Lay noodles on pan, layer accordingly.
  8. Top with mozerella cheese.
  9. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes. Serve hot
