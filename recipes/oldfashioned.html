Best Old Fashioned Recipe

The best old fashioned you'll ever drink!

This is a recipe that's been my favorite after a hard week's work where I'm wanting to just relax, or show off for guests.

  • 4 oz of your favorite bourbon or rye. Personally I recommend Bulliet or Buffalo Trace
  • 1/4 tsp aromatic bitters
  • 1 sugar cube
  • An orange slice or peel for garnishing
  • Ice is optional if you'd like it on the rocks.

The prep is simple. Just follow the directions below:

  1. Place the sugar cube into the bottom of the glass and drip the aromatic bitters on top of it.
  2. Begin gently mashing the sugar cube into the bottom of the glass.
  3. Pour in your whisky of choice.
  4. Stir until sugar content is dissolved.
  5. Twist orange or orange peel into the glass.
  6. Place ice into the glass gently to avoid splashing and losing any of your deliciious drink.
  7. Enjoy!
