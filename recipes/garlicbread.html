Garlic Bread

The best garlic bread you'll ever eat!

This is a recipe that's been passed down in my family for generations. I'll keep it short and sweet for you. You only really need a couple ingredients.

  • 1 loaf of french bread
  • 1 stick of butter, preferably softened
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese

The prep is simple. Just follow the directions below:

  1. Grab a small bowl you can mix your ingredients together in.
  2. Mash the softened butter with garlic, Italian seasoning, and parmesan cheese.
  3. Slice the loaf of french bread in half horizontally.
  4. Sprinkle on parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes.
  6. Put sheet under the broiler on high for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Cut in 1 inch thick slices and serve hot.
  8. Enjoy!
